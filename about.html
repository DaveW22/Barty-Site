About Barty

Smarter shopping. Real savings. Built for everyday life.

Barty helps people cut through the noise of modern grocery shopping by comparing prices, optimising baskets, simplifying loyalty, and showing exactly where savings can be made.

In a world of changing prices, fragmented offers, and rising costs, Barty exists to give power back to the shopper. Save money with clarity, not effort.

Basket-level comparison over marketing noise and headline discounts.
Transparent savings visibility before checkout, not after.
Built to reduce overspending while keeping shopping decisions simple.

The Problem

Shopping has become harder than it should be

Everyday shoppers are expected to navigate:

  • Constantly changing supermarket prices
  • Complicated loyalty schemes and offers
  • Different prices across multiple retailers
  • The pressure to shop smarter with less time

Most people do not lack effort. They lack visibility. Barty was built to solve that.

What Barty Does

A simpler, smarter way to shop

With Barty, users can:

  • Compare supermarket prices clearly
  • Optimise a shopping list across one or multiple stores
  • See actual savings versus an unoptimised shop
  • Store loyalty cards in one place
  • Make better-value switches and substitutions
  • Build a more efficient way to shop over time

No confusion. No wasted spend. No unnecessary friction.

Our Belief

Intelligence should benefit the customer

Retailers use pricing systems, data models, and behavioural insights every day. We believe shoppers deserve tools that work just as hard for them.

Barty exists to rebalance that relationship by using intelligent systems to help users make informed, cost-saving decisions with confidence.

Designed for Trust

Built for users, not incentives

We work for the user, not the retailer. That means:

  • No hidden bias toward any store
  • No manipulated outcomes for commission-first decisions
  • No dark patterns
  • No misleading savings claims
  • Clear, honest results users can understand quickly

Trust is not a marketing message. It is the product standard.

Privacy & Data

Respect by design

  • Collect only the data needed to make the service work
  • Give users control over what they add and keep
  • Never sell personal data
  • Use strong security practices across the platform
  • Align with UK GDPR and modern privacy expectations

Where data helps improve the platform, we prioritise anonymised and aggregated insights wherever possible.
