Privacy Policy

Your data, handled with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how Barty collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you visit the Barty website or join the waitlist.

Last updated: April 15, 2026

What this policy covers

This policy applies to the Barty marketing website, including the waitlist signup form and any pre-launch updates sent in connection with that form. It covers information you provide directly and limited technical information generated when you use the site.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Your email address when you join the Barty waitlist.
  • Basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and server logs collected by hosting or form-processing providers.
  • Any information you choose to share if you reply to a Barty email or contact us directly.

How we use your information

  • To manage the waitlist and send launch, access, or product-related updates.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website.
  • To respond to requests, questions, or support messages.
  • To understand early interest in Barty at an aggregated level.

Legal bases

Where applicable, we rely on your consent when you submit your email for waitlist communications, and on legitimate interests to keep the site secure, prevent misuse, and improve how the service is presented.

Sharing and service providers

We may share information with trusted providers that help us run the site and waitlist, such as website hosting and form-processing tools. For example, the current waitlist form is submitted through Formspree. We do not sell personal data.

Cookies and tracking

The site does not currently advertise the use of personalised advertising or analytics cookies. However, basic technical logs and essential service-provider processing may still occur as part of delivering the website and submitting forms.

Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including managing the waitlist, meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and maintaining security records.

Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the use of your personal data, and in some cases request a copy of it. You may also withdraw consent for waitlist emails at any time by using the unsubscribe option in those messages.

Data security

We use reasonable organisational and technical measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, but we aim to keep data handling proportionate and responsible.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the Barty service develops. When we make material changes, we will update the date on this page and publish the revised version here.

Contact and requests

If you have a privacy question or want to exercise a data right, contact Barty using the contact details provided on this website or in communications sent by Barty.
